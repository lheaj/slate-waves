
> An imposing glass dome 60 feet tall overlooks a sprawling marketplace. Shops and buildings are of exquisite making, with intricate decorations representing flowers and the marine wildlife, but all is covered by an oppressing layer of moss, rust, and decadence. Owners of the shops, dressed in clean shirts with Papillions and braces, carry all a look of awareness toward their own clients. Few items are exposed, the ones in display are mostly weapons, potions and food and drinks.

Pressure Point Plaza is the neuralgic centre of [[Watersafe]], both before and after its demise. Defended by a copious amount of traps and riddled doors, the trading square offers safe heaven from the blighted infesting the undefended areas of the city. Within the Plaza numerous shops call for tourists and business travellers to take a peak at their wares

[[Pressure Cup]] is a cafe' offering eccentric Watersafe-themed drinks as the BlightPA beer, Blue wine, Affogato Martini etc. 

Jewels can be bought and sold at [[Jojo's Corals]], where an exquisite collection of illegally extracted reefs are encased into precious blends of silver and bronze.

If one's instead looking for off-table weaponry, [[The Sword in the Sand]] displays an enticing collection of recently enchanted weapons. The owner Rico Rupet, a permanently tipsy gnome with a pressing gambling issue, is drinking himself away from misery to avoid thinking of his debt to [[Ratzlovich]].
