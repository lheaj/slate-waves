[[Borbor Roctofellor]]'s residence is built in stone and timber, it has a round collection of roofs faintly coloured in a forest green that makes the building jump out from the rest on the street. The finishing on the large iron gates and the wooden doors indicate expert craftmanship went into the construction. Multiple chimneys exudes grey smoke.

Two hired muscles are looking after the mansion when BorBor is away. The two are Orcs of moderate intellect and are currently playing a gambling game of cards sat on two stools right outside the main gates.

The house has only a single entrance from the front door.

#### The Main Hall

The main hall is wide and extends on a single floor with high ceilings. The building from outside gives the impression of multiple floors but its heights is just a preference for magnificence from its creator.

The hall is empty and silent, there are animal heads adorning the walls and several other hunting trophies and sporting memorabilia.

[[Denise The Maid]], a stout Warforged, works at the residence and is currently cleaning an upside closet. She allows no stains or even a grain of dust to sit within the mansion. She is a perfectionists and will go bad mad if anything is destroyed or soiled. She will get into the room immediately if she hears noises. She doubles as a property guard due to her combat prowess and some weapons BorBor installed on her.

A small pet [[Bombling]] also waddles around the hall. If attacked or if the Bombling decides to attack, it will explode and have the effect of a [[Bombast]] after which it will go in disuse but still remain alive. The bomb is generally curious and acts as a toddler would with strangers.

#### BorBor Workshop

The workshop contains a number of unfinished weapons and other gadgets BorBor is experimenting on. The players can find:

- A Picture of BorBor and [[Garabatta]]. The glass is broken on the frame but they are clearly smiling as they cut the ribbon of a shop together. They are hugging, and look happy.
- A “Work Order” from someone addressed to BorBor.
- With a successful investigation check DC 14, one can notice another explosive device being made. It is unfinished, resembles a bomb but has small appendices that close and open, resembling a ship cannons. They look connected to a two layers core, one of the layers seems dirties with powder, the other seems wet with water.

In this room the players will also find their reward:

- One handgun
- One Boombast
- [**_Pipe of Smoke Monsters_**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9228924-pipe-of-smoke-monsters)
- A small chest containing 100 pieces of gold, it needs to be unlocked by a
