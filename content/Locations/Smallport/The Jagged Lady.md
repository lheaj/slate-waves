> *Dark oak and Mahogany contour ample arches and painted glass doors to a dimly lit, busy, bar. The air is rich with strong brown coffee, mixing along the pungent reek of absinth breadth and stale olive margaritas. The odd, thunderous, blast of hot air from the bar’s mixology steamer occasionally mutes the jazz trio playing forgotten in the background.*

The small eateries situated in a backstreet nearby [[The Smallport Market]], is frequented by both the high-class merchants of [[Smallport]] and its criminal circles, often purposely mixing amidst and assortment of eccentric cocktails and jazz music.
