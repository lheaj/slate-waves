### Environment:

 Once a sprawling underwater metropolis, a marvel of human ingenuity and ambition, hidden beneath the ocean's surface. This submarine city, is a place where art deco architecture meets the eerie glow of bioluminescent sea life.

The city is encased in massive glass domes, reinforced with steel and adorned with intricate designs. The streets are lined with towering skyscrapers, their facades decorated with ornate carvings and gilded accents. Neon signs flicker in the dim light, casting an otherworldly glow on the once bustling streets below.

As you wander through the city, you notice the constant hum of machinery and the rhythmic pulse of the water pumps that keep the city alive. The air is thick with the scent of saltwater and oil, a reminder of the delicate balance that sustains this underwater haven.

### Population:

Once ordinary citizens, they are now victims of a corrupted alchemical experiment gone awry. Each individual bears the marks of their transformation, their bodies and minds altered in ways both terrifying and extraordinary.

The people of the city are a diverse mix of scientists, artists, and laborers, all drawn to the promise of a new life beneath the waves. But the alchemical experiments have left them with grotesque mutations and uncanny abilities. Some have skin that glows with an eerie bioluminescence, while others have limbs that have morphed into mechanical appendages, a fusion of flesh and metal.

These alterations come with a price. The once vibrant community is now a shadow of its former self, with many residents struggling to cope with their new forms. Some have developed telepathic abilities, able to read the thoughts of those around them, but at the cost of their own sanity. Others can manipulate the water around them, creating powerful currents and waves, but their bodies are slowly deteriorating from the strain.
