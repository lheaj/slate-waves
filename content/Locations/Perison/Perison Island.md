> “The sun is blurred behind grey clouds melting into a conically shaped mound of an island. Multitudes of construction levers sit among towering chimneys breathing deathly thick fogs, engulfing the sparse houses below. The buildings made of stone and wood look bent and broken by time and violence. Gaping holes the size of elephants expose the privacy of dining families and bedrooms to rain and crime.”

The island is a gloomy and dark place, the landmark is littered with mining refuse and pollution from the smelting of a large [[CO7]] deposit. The polluted air is more than just combustion, [[Violet - The Witch of Perison]], the local ruler, poisons the air with a mind-numbing substance that make people lose their will and forget about their dreams. Any creature breathing the Perison fog has constant disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws.

“_The fog intrudes your lungs, inescapable. You feel light-headed and slowly feel your determination fade into the back of your identity, forgotten.”_

A group of rebels called "[[The Dreamers]]” managed to uncover Violet’s secret. They began living below ground to avoid breathing the polluted air, and are planning to steer a rebellion.
