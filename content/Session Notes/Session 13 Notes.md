### Opening Monolouge

### The Descent into [[Watersafe]]

- Throughout the descent into deeper levels of Watersafe, **noised can be heard echoing for far away chambers**, carried through the resonating brass and steel plumbing and pillars. Sounds of distant laughs seem sometimes as near as a few feet away. The giggles are broken by occasional slurping and munching.

- As the group moves through the glass tunnels, **the electrical circuit gives out** repeatedly for a few seconds indicating issues with the electric supply further down the line.

#### Scene - The Descent
>*“You enter a small single-room submarine. An oval round window exposes the ocean with its horizon right above a steering wheel and a cock-pit rife with levers. The vehicle is lit with two cylindrical lamps occasionally emitting a puff of white smoke. Brass tubes emanating heat adorn the walls and two brown leather sofas are placed at the sides of the command position."

Highlights:
- *The levers and steering wheel* - Who is driving? (Check to navigate)
- *Controls and engine.* There seem to be a few red-lights on with unequivocal symbols indicating issues with the vehicle.
- *The smoke coming out of the lights* and the occasional drip of boiling CO7 dripping through the pipes.

#### Scene - The Welcome Room
>*“You descend in from the enter pipe-way into a windowless room with wooden floor and a single blinking lightbulb. The room is covered with “Welcome” signs, a few of which have fallen off or are hanging askew to their original place. The objects in the room, including a desk, chair and parchments seem all to have been violently broken and scattered across the room.”*

Highlights:

-          *A video player,* malfunctioning
-          *A video cassette* containing [[Propaganda video for Watersafe]]
-          (hidden) traces of blood lead to the glass tunnels
#### Scene - The Glass Tunnel
>*“A long, single direction, tunnel continues ahead, its walls are entirely made of glass held together by melded strips of brass. Outside, the lights of an underwater city faintly glow, drawing the shapes of building and moving marine wildlife of similar size.”*

Highlights
-          (hidden) traces of blood. A hand-print on the glass.
-          Visible far away, there seems to be a hall of glass emanating light as if the electrical systems were turned on. (The lighted area is the [[Pressure Point Plaza]])
-          There are creaks in the glass in certain spots. 
#### Scene - The Written Hall
>*“You enter a dark hall of iron walls, with two large windows at each side giving sights into the blue ocean. A fountain with statues of bare-chested women sits at the centre of the hall. The water has filled what seems to be a lower floor up to the current level. You can hear a harsh sound as if a rock is being scraped against the iron walls. Its cadence is slow and irregular.”

Highlights
-          The water covering the middle of the room is still. (Perception 13) Occasionally, ripples will form on its surface.
-          The hall is covered in elvish scriptures, written with a fluorescent purple ink (DC 13, the ink is [[Brightblood]]). Single punctuated sentences. “I came to learn. I drew the laugh. I love to breath. The blood you cast”
-          The scraping sound at the end of the room. In the same direction, a set of stairs leads to a lower level, currently submerged by the fountain overflowing waters.
-          The way underwater is closed by a faulty door.

> *Pale beyond skin tones of the living, a man is writing symbols and letters against a wall. He's using his bare finger to write, emitting a loud screech at the contact with the wall, with a long nail bathed in a purple fluid. He is wearing a tattered tweed jacket and a white shirt with a black bowtie. Purple fluorescent webbing, like engorged veins, emanate from his eyes onto his whole body*

The creature writing on the walls is [[Dr. Richard Rhemors]]. Richard has bearing the [[Brightblood]]
infection now for more than a decade. As very few could, he tamed the innate urge to laugh and give in to the aggression and instead harnessed the power of the disease to, according to him, reach a higher level of human potential. In truth, his mind is just as consumed as the other [[Brightblood Infected]], although his limited self-awareness grants him an exceptional control over the body enhancing prowess of Brightblood. The Dr. holds a **key*** to open the glass door to the lower levels. The door is underwater and the surroundings are littered with infected. Several [[Brightblood Infected]] hide beneath the waters covering the stairs to the lower level. Richard has control over them.
#### Scene The submerged lock

Outside the water, up from stone stairs, dim lights flicker from iron lanterns contouring the details of a multitude of merchant boxes casting their stiff shadows in what seems to be a warehouse. The sound of jaws snapping on soft tissues can be heard in the distance, occasionally broken by erratic laughs.

> A figure, its back to you, rests crouched behind one of the boxes, spying ahead.

The Hangar is littered with [[Brightblood infected]].

The figure crouched is [[Jazeer Long Look]]. She is hiding from the infected while looking for her boss [[Gideon Blackbirch]]. The two, along with another pair of thugs, were traversing the town to reach [[Pressure Point Plaza]] but have been driven into hiding by the [[Brightblood Infected]].

Gideon is up ahead, fighting infected in the dark gunning madly at his flesh, using a rapier and a gun to scare them off.. Two of his employees lay on the ground, bite marks and ripped flesh wounds across their body.
