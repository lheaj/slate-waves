 Born out of a perilous blend of concentrated wealth, ennui, and a desperate struggle for survival, the annual event strikes a precarious balance between the prospect of immense riches and the looming threat of death. 
 
 The spectacle, hosted for the entertainment of all [[Smallport]], envisions pitting a cast of willing participants against intricate riddles and deadly perils designed to break their bodies and minds, all for the temporary amusement of the citizens.

The grand prize: 20,000 gold pieces to the first to conquer all the challenges. Funded by the affluent, the Curator’s Challenge offers a much-needed respite from the drudgery of daily life and a fleeting chance for the desperate to transform their fortunes.
