The term "Cataclysm" often describes the fateful 40-day period when relentless, world-shattering rains poured from cloudless skies, engulfing much of the known world. The torrential downpours struck homes and cities with such force that those recounting the event through word-of-mouth likened the deluge to towering, crashing waves.

Nothing is known about the truthful provenience of such an unexplained weather disaster. Some are still studying it as a scientific matter while others chose to explain it as a punishment from the Gods of the old world.
