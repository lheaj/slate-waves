Violet, the Witch of Perison, was deeply engrossed in her papers, her mind occupied with matters far removed from the understanding of the common folk of the steam city. The party engaged in a prolonged dialogue with her, unravelling the complexities of her decisions as ruler. She confessed to clouding Roderick's mind and severing ties to her childhood, all in the name of what she believed was best for the island—to spur work and commerce. However, her choices also unleashed corruption and disease. She justified her path, weighing one evil against another, ultimately resorting to dark magic.

A heated debate ensued between [Violet - The Witch of Perison](Actors/Violet%20-%20The%20Witch%20of%20Perison.md) and [Gosha](Characters/Gosha.md) over morality and choices. Marlin, with his clever stories, managed to charm the witch into revealing more than intended. She spoke of her mysterious connection to "[The Wizard](Actors/The%20Wizard)", naming him *Fiel*, the sinister figure responsible for abducting youths in [[Smallport]]. Violet disclosed unsettling truths about her former instructor—a man consumed by his obsession with youth, whose immense power rendered him unreachable by any ordinary group of adventurers.

Unable to reach an accord, Violet decided to resolve the confrontation through force. A fierce battle erupted, with spells and unconventional weapons flying about. Vanessa, seizing the moment, leapt onto her flying broom and delivered a precise, close-range headshot to Violet’s skull, ending the tyrant's reign. Yet, as the witch fell, the group found themselves pulled into another plane of existence. There, they were unwilling witnesses to Violet's dark choices and the price she paid for her power. Spectres from otherworldly realms descended upon her lifeless soul, ready to tear it apart.

Without hesitation, Marlin, despite his fragile frame, expanded his body to carry Violet back to the material plane, rescuing her from eternal torment. Touched by his act of selflessness, Violet experienced a profound change of heart. Recognizing the failure of her methods and the triumph of valor, she chose to follow [[Roderick]]'s guidance. The party watched as the two reconciled, heralding the liberation of Perison from its dark chapter.

With Perison freed, the group looked ahead to their next adventure. [[Vanessa The Vulture]] had unfinished business—a sister to find in the city beneath the ocean.
