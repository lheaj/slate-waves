The group set out for the Witch's Manor, a foreboding structure perched atop a rocky outcrop overlooking a vast canyon. Despite its ominous reputation, the stronghold of the tyrant of [[Perison Island]] appeared eerily unguarded, perhaps the witch’s magic was all the protection she needed. Seizing the opportunity, the party slipped through a secondary entrance that led directly to the prison cells. It was there that they encountered the jailors of [[Gosha]]'s sister, a deadly sniper and a fearsome, fully-armored guard wielding a chainsaw blade.

After a fierce battle, the group triumphed, aided by an unexpected ally, the Bugbear [Jhollho!t](Actors/Jhollho!t), newly freed from captivity. Together, they ascended the manor's winding stairs to the upper levels, where they faced the Witch's accountants. With cunning negotiation and a touch of deceit, they secured several valuable items before continuing their ascent to the Witch’s tower room.
