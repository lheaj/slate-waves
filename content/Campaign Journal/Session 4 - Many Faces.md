
[[Garabatta]]’s thirst for vengeance burned hot as dragonfire. The goblin was hellbent on exacting justice upon Baldie the dwarf, whose hands were stained with the mystery of a vanished halfling—Garbatta’s lost love. Vanessa, with a voice that could cow the fiercest warrior, managed to harness the goblin's seething rage. She turned her fearsome gaze upon Baldie, demanding answers about the elusive "white rabbit" card. Faced with the lethal glint of steel poised by his eye, Baldie’s courage crumbled and he divulged his role as a weapon forger for shadowy factions. His only contact was a roguishly handsome young man, and his shipments were destined for Perison Island. Each collection was made by a one-eyed female Tiefling cloaked in a gray mantle.

With their prize information in hand, the group confronted [[Marco]] about discrepancies in his guidance. Distrust lingered like a morning mist, but they chose to follow him into the next chamber. A vast library unfurled before them, but it was not as silent as it should have been. Masked figures—faces of bears and foxes—rummaged through the tomes, led by a Tiefling whose description mirrored Baldie's.

[[Vanessa The Vulture]] stood firm as the Tiefling’s eyes narrowed, initially mistaking her for another. Realization struck when Marco’s disguise fell away. Swiftly, Marco seized a hidden weapon, and chaos erupted. The Tiefling, [[Jo - The Brass Rat,]] demonstrated deadly prowess, her magic bullets and poisoned blades slicing through the air. She maimed the courageous druid and severed Thunderfoot’s arm. Yet as the battle turned against her, she made a desperate escape through the window.

In the aftermath, Marco led the party to a secret vault, pilfering a precious heart-shaped gem. He swore its significance in finding his friend, apologizing for his deceptions. [[Gosha]]’s eyes widened, recognizing the gem from a divine vision. Doubt shadowed the party’s thoughts, but they ventured with Marco through a concealed passage to his ship. The horizon called them forth to [[Perison Island]], where destiny awaited.
