While navigating their journey, the boat's all-in-one engineer-captain-deckboy [[Bipzip]] delivered the rather alarming news that the fuel engine had gone ka-boom in a spectacular fashion. Without a fresh tank filled with the precious [[CO7]], their boat was dead in the water.

Determined and resourceful, the group set their sights on a nearby island, hoping to find the fuel they needed. Upon landing, they encountered an unexpectedly chill hermit crab who was busy collecting an assortment of trinkets and precious refuse.

This eccentric crustacean directed them toward the heart of the island. Their path was far from peaceful, as they soon found themselves facing a band of goblin buccaneers and two not-so-friendly crocodiles, Bert and Roger. After an intense showdown, our heroes emerged victorious.

Their journey took a pleasant turn when they stumbled upon a driad village. The friendly and carefree villagers offered their warm hospitality, and for once, even the usually grumpy Thunderfoot found the environment tolerable. But their quest was far from over.

As they ventured deeper into the island's dark, thick forest, they came upon the main group of goblins. These mischievous creatures were in the midst of presenting the coveted fuel tank to their menacing lady patron, a dreadful Harpy.
