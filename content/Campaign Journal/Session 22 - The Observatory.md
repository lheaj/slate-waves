By exploring the other room within the Observatory, the group found a secret elevator leading to secret cellar cave. Before diving down into what seemed to be Richard's hiding spot, Gosha manged to find Jhollo'th lost son, who carried an item he'd found in Watersafe, dropped out of a pillow, and hence previously stolen by Mr. Soren. A sword, named Wavelight, a long lost weapon harbouring the might of the seas.

Marlin uncovered a stitched-up body within a morgue room. After researching nearby notes on how to wake the creature, he decided to rise the monster who turned out ot be just a scared golem, happy to share information about its fragmented memories and the traps within the cave down below.

The party entered the elevator leading to a pitch-black stone corridor. Following the instruction of the flesh golem, they found a room rife with golems and parts, ready to be awaken, and managed to prepare for an incoming fight.

Progressing trough a small backdoor corridor, they took Richard by surprise. After a deadly fight which involved a copious amount of fireballs, an enraged golem, and a heroic last stand by Gosha and his gleaming sword attracting enemies blows to protect his party, the group managed to vanquish Richard and his plans for global dominion.
