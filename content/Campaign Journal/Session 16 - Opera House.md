At the Arcade, Vanessa overheard what sounded like Jazeer, in the company of two other voices, discussing the kidnapping of Princess La'Ture. The group confronted [[Ratzlovich]] about his loan business and after a solid beat down got him into promising never to pursue extortion again. To be safe, Thunderoo ate Ratzlovich debtors list, so that he may never find them again. 

Regina didn't consider the whole ordeal as "fun", and demanded the group to go to the Opera house for some proper enjoyment. The abandoned theatre was rumoured to be home to a monstrous creature, but Regina insisted she went there often and never saw anything amiss. Here, utilizing the stage space and lighting, Gosha created a spectacular and moving water show about fish living through hardship similar to what the party has gone to. Thunderfoot instead recited an Australian poem for Regina. Just as he was about to end, a deep and aged voice broke the performance. The group met Lumiere, once a man, star of the Opera, he was victim of an accident near [[Lumina Spire]]. He recounted the night when a laboratory explosion engulfed him with light and disfigured his face and body, permanently ending his career, but giving him immense power in the process. Lumiere was victim of an acute form of [[Brightblood]]. Prisoner of his madness, he attacked the group, and met his end.

[[Regina Tusseaux]], then admitting the night had been worthy enough to be defined as "fun", invited the group back to [[Hotel Tusseaux]] and to her yearly Ball, being hosted just a few days later.

On the way back to the hotel, Regina and Gosha exchanged secrets and memories, smiles and intense looks.

Once at the hotel, Regina brought the group to the Luxury Suites floor. Their room had a thermal bath, velvet sofas and all the amenities an adventurer could ever wish for. There, she also mentioned her newfound friend [[Princess La'Ture]], travelling with a wizard friend of hers. Marlin jumped at the occasion and asked to meet the Wizard and Princess right there and then.

Regina brought Marlin to the room, but only Princess was there. At the hint of her sister's presence she recoiled in anger and showed her hurtful misconception that Vanessa was behind her kidnapping.

On his way back to his room, Marlin spotted a book brimming with magic energy. He "traded" it there and then for a rusty key he found on an unimportant floor back in Perison Island.

With the tome hidden beneath his wizardly gown, Marlin pondered his next move, as everything he was looking for might just be now in his hands
