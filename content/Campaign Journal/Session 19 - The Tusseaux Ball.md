Still moved from the events within [[The Book of Time]], the group readied themselves for a night to remember at the Tusseaux Ball. Many of their targets would be there. Gideon was certain to make an appearance, along with [[Princess La'Ture]] and [[The Wizard]].

After getting a top-level fashion haircut by the scissor master Thunderfoot, the group proceeded to approach the hall downstairs. Inside, a luxurious buffet and sweet live music invited rich and famous guests into an opulent room, symbol of [[Watersafe]] long lost splendor.

Gosha quickly spotted [[Jhollho?v]], the little Bugbear lost son of their barbarian companion. The youth was enjoying his freedom, tending tables, away from the safety measure his father often implemented, and got lost again back into the crowd.

Meanwhile, Thom bumped into Marco, hiding among the crowd, probably an uninvited presence, in his quest to find his long lost friend.

Standing closely to a grand Piano, [[The Wizard]] waved at Marlin, as if waiting for his arrival. The two engaged into a vivid conversation about theft, life, and knowledge, which ended in the The Wizard offering an apprenticeship that Marlin quickly turned down.

The music stopped, as a sudden blast cracked the dancing floor, propelling a few guests right to the ceiling. A masked figure lifted from the darkness atop a flying Guardian Construct. Bandits, wearing iron masks shaped like various animals, crowded the room and started stripping guests of their possessions, clearly searching for a specific piece of jewellery, a necklace.

The group refused to comply, defended Regina and the other guests and battled the scoundrels. During the fight, Marco unexpectedly tried to defend the masked leader, even to the point of shooting and attacking his own comrades fighting that specific bandit. The Wizard meanwhile, continued his discourse with the fairy mage, and occasionally helped during the battle. His spells reverberated through the opulent room, shattering the construct to pieces with immense ease while keeping playing the grand piano.

Once the battle resolved, the wizard quickly spotted Marco and his [[The Heart of Desire]]. He obtained the artifact, almost suffocating Marco in the process. Speaking to Marlin, he explained how he has been hunting for an explanation to the rains that drowned the world in the new seas, wanting knowledge and stopping at nothing to obtain it. For that he needed years, time, strength, and people's desires and willpowers, which he flatly stole and ripped from his victims. The last one was meant to be Princess, which was important to him for her purity and clear desires. But as he was about to consumer her life and essence, Vanessa stepped in front of her, taking the deadly spell on herself, withering to her cold and excruciating death.

Opening a portal into other dimensions he showed secrets and dark Gods, finally he opened a window into a sky island and finally made his offer to follow him to uncover the mysteries of this world's curse and cataclysms. Gosha, taken by the ire of Vanessa's demise, double-flying-kicked the Wizard into the portal, sending him flying through the sky on the other side. Thom and Marco had a quick squabble where each tried to fling the other through the portal but both failed (phew).

As the group was catching their breath from the last encounter, they turned in horror as a flood of infected invaded the room from the ground hole and commenced to banquet on the terrified guests.
