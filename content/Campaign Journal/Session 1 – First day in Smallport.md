
The group gathered at the [The Jagged Lady](The%20Jagged%20Lady.md) at the behest of [[Marco]]. Each member had independently agreed to search for Marco’s missing friend, motivated by the promise of safe passage to other island nations to pursue their personal goals.

After a brief inquiry with Marco about his missing companion, the party decided to explore the town until the “Curator’s Challenge”—a deadly trial where the poor and desperate risked their lives in a bid to reverse their fortunes while providing entertainment for the wealthy spectators.

Their exploration led them to the bustling Smallport market, where they encountered [[Aeramellhar]] (Ari), an eccentric apothecary who sold them a variety of potions. During their visit, a man transformed into a savage badger by a potion mishap sought revenge on Ari but was swiftly dealt with by the group.

[[Garabatta]], a goblin, one among the richest member of the Smallport merchant community, promised to help the group with whatever within his means to thank them for saving his dear friend, the apothecary.

Seeking respite and more information, the adventurers made their way to the [[Rivet Recovery]]. There they met [[Dr. Vera Steel]], the head doctor, who asked for their help in investigating a mysterious, rabid individual causing chaos within the hospital, forcing an evacuation of the patients.

Inside the clinic, the group encountered two individuals, seemingly mad with joy and exuding a fluorescent liquid from their eyes, threaded with engorged capillary veins. The group engaged these strange beings in combat, aiming to rescue an elderly couple trapped in a patient room.
