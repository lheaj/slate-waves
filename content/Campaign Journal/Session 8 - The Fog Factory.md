Waking up in a sewage canal is never an ideal start to the day, but Marlin and Thom managed to make the best of it. They engaged in merry chats and a polite wrestling match, each offering gold to the other in exchange for self-serenity and righteousness. Thom emerged victorious, and Marlin returned to tinkering with arcane knowledge, occasionally lighting up the sewage dormitories with unchecked tongues of fire and discovering new helpful spells.

Vanessa spent some time enquiring about the missing person she’s been searching for, but to no avail. She did, however, learn that Jo, the mechanical Tiefling tied to the white bunny card, was also in town and working with the dreaded violet witch.

The group then attended a meeting of the rebels, [[the Dreamers]], where a heated discussion about the best course of action took place. Fanger argued for striking the Fog Factory to break the machine producing the mind-numbing fog and save the people of Perison from the witch's control. [[Roderick]], on the other hand, shared his history and familiarity with the witch, mentioning that she wasn’t always an evil tyrant. He proposed a plan to investigate her identity by visiting her old house. Elias suggested setting fire to the witch’s mansion and taking the fight head-on. After a brief discussion, the group decided to attack the Fog Factory, weaken the witch’s hold on Perison, and put her on the defensive. Marlin, with a touch of magic, convinced [[Roderick]] to join the mission. [[The Dreamers]]' leader, a veteran warrior, would prove to be a valuable ally on the battlefield.

The group navigated through the sewage channels to find a back entrance to the Fog Factory. There, they encountered an unexpected presence: a steam mephit from the fourth layer of the Nine Hells. Attracted by the deranged nature of Perison Island and the steams around the Fog Factory, the creature sought some old-fashioned sadistic fun. Marlin seized the opportunity, convincing the mephit to spy for the group and reveal the positions of the witch’s workers. In exchange, the mephit could play mischievous pranks on the factory guards and workers. One such prank involved Vanessa vaulting over a side wall and descending with both knives onto the neck of an armed machinist. The mephit found the ordeal exhilarating.

The party fought their way through the factory's lower floor and proceeded to the upstairs room. There, Vanessa eavesdropped on a conversation between one of the witch’s workers and a familiar voice, where the latter left no room for negotiation. The orders were clear: squeeze more money from the people of Perison to fund the fog machinery and weapons, or the “organization” would abandon the witch and her entourage.

Moments after the witch’s minion left the room, a fight erupted between the group, the remaining workers, and [[Jo - The Brass Rat,]]. Jo was as fierce an adversary as ever, but  Marlin might now have permanently forgotten that after a large ice rock propelled by Jo’s knee-cannon knocked him out cold on the factory floor...
