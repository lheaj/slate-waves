After finding Thom the Tuba playing a rather sad and uninspired tune to a somewhat interested crowd, the group proceeded to hunt for the Captain pendant, questioning the boat owner Axel Goldtime. After some fortunate persuasion, Axel agreed to show the pendant within his treasure room so that the group could help him pick up a new price for his gambling games. 

In the safe, coin was abundant, along with trinkets, the pendant, and a giant cyclops accountant working on the nights earning. Understanding the cyclops was there on his own accord and not as math-slave, wasn't easy, but everyone got there in the end. As the group was about to acquire the pendant, a host Sahuagin barons attacked the ship looking for the very same jewl and it's weather-controlling powers. Dispatching the fishmen wasn't easy but with the aid of jigsaw-blades and ratatoille-style controlled cyclops, the group managed to quash the threat. 

In the end, Sage the bodyguard got fired for being useless; the cyclops went back to his favourite past time (counting coins, he prefers that even to sudoku); Thom proposed to [[Vexi Crinkle]] (the middle-aged goblin lady with a penchant for cheap cigarettes and strong men) and convinced her to join the group and their journey, abandoning her debts and the seven husbands with their seven different accents. The band of ragged adventurers headed back to their ship for a good night rest, hoping to sleep off the drugged drinks.
