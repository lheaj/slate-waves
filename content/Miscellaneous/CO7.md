A slate grey, viscous substance capable of easy ignition. If combined and treated through the correct scientific process, the fuel is capable of generating reactions with a variety of outcomes, often mistaken for magic.

CO7 can be extracted in its purest form from underwater deposits or liquified from rare earth particles present in few known locations across the [[Slate Waves]], among these, [[Perison Island]] is one of the most prolific islands containing several extraction mines.
