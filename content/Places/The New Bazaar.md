_“A torrent of people from every species inundates clear cobble-stone streets overcrowded with their tangible excitement, perusing through a labyrinth of eccentric market stands. The loud chanting of merchants calls every passerby to take a peek at everything from heavy artillery to home-made dream cakes.”_

Bustling with life, the market is the vibrant heart of [[Smallport]], pulsing with the lifeblood of commerce and [[CO7]]. Over time, the hodgepodge of stands evolved into structured shops, carpeting the area with narrow, capillary alleys for curious buyers to navigate and explore the diverse wares on display.

Ever teeming with activity, the market attracts all kinds of buyers, including those seeking items not sold elsewhere due to safety or legality concerns. Hosting approximately 65-70 shops at any given time—depending on the season and the fortunes of local business owners—the market offers a treasure trove of goods. From everyday items like food and simple weapons to rare crafts imbued with magic or innovative warring technologies, the market has it all.

The market opens daily, without exceptions or holidays, from the crack of dawn until the sun dips beyond the sea.

To the east of the market, a gentle rise leads to the residential area of Smallport, where small single-family homes line narrow alleys along a winding uphill road. Further up, a few houses stand out for their size and rich décor, some resembling small palaces.
