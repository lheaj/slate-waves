The Rivet Recovery is located in [Smallport](Smallport.md). A complex of buildings erected on stone bricks and brass pillars, the hospital is the only medical facility within Smallport and one of the largest across the neighbouring islands. 

The clinic does not distinguish from citizen of passers-by and all cures and medication are extended free of charge to its patients. The costs are generously covered by the clinic's patrons, the affluent merchants of Smallport, who regard the hospital as a necessity to foster the town's economic and commercial prosperity.

At the helm of the Rivet Recovery is [Dr. Vera Steel](Actors/Dr.%20Vera%20Steel), who leads the [[Order of the Brass Cast ]], a group of skilled and compassionate medics who work at the Rivet Recovery and devote their life to its patients. Their tireless devotion has established the hospital as the centre of advanced medical research, spear-heading research into deep and complex illness. The clinic's treatment of [[Rustlung]] and [[Metallosis]] are at the pinnacle of medical progress across the [[Slate Waves]].
