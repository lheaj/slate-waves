Finding a friend – Uprise of the Menagerie

Embark on an electrifying odyssey with Marco, as he hunts for a long-lost comrade amidst the industrial roar of engines and the relentless ticking of clockwork in a smog-choked, nameless world, shattered into fragments. Every island breeds its own brand of cunning, where the shrewd and resourceful rise to power and those who falter are swiftly left to their fates.

Steel yourself to gather unimaginable wealth, unleash a hail of bullets (or fireballs, your choice), topple tyrants, and mingle with society's outcasts. Do it all your way—be it through blazing trails or quiet sabotage. In this tumultuous world, the true treasure lies in the friendships you build.

Sometimes, a friend is all we need. Often, we lose a few along the way, stop seeing them, or feeling their presence. They might look different or speak in ways we no longer recognize. Yet, they often remain, just beneath the surface.

**What:** Playing D&D 5e 2024

**Where:** We aim to play 3 session Online every 4, with at least, roughly, once a month in person session.

The Online sessions will be played on Roll20.

The in-person sessions will be played in London at RPG Taverns (Colab Tavern), south of London Bridge (10-15m walk).

**When:** Start around mid-Jan 2025. Weekly. Each week we’ll then organize through a poll in advance for the weeks after, to find best attendance date.

We’ll play mostly weekdays when online, start time 20:00 for about 3.5 hours. When in person we could end up playing over the weekend too, depending on availabilities.

We’ll play as long as 3 players (at least) are available each week, usually means we’ll play every week. If you miss a session, don’t worry at all! You character won’t perish, and we’ll give you a summary of what happened on the next session!

**Duration:** The campaign is designed to last about 7-9 months. Could be a bit less or a bit more. Please consider the vague commitment when signing up!

**Audio/Video:** I’ll have my Video on, and most of the party will too, so we’d like to play with Video. But if you want to have only voice some or most times it’s absolutely fine!

**Cost:** Free, I don’t ask for payment for DMing or resources, but we will play once a month in a paid venue which will cost 10-15 GBP each.

**Playstyle Details:**

-        **Steampunk Setting:** With a variety of biomes and environments. Character heavy storyline.

-        **50/50 Combat-Roleplay** balance: Adapting to what the group likes best and depending on in-game problem solving tactics.

-        **Deadly difficulty**: This campaign has no plot armour. Of course, as long as everybody has fun along the way.

-        **Rule of cool:** If you can roleplay it well, we’ll find a way to represent it mechanically in the game. If you roleplay it poorly we’ll try anyways my friend don’t worry, the important thing is trying and having fun.

-        **Flavour is free:** We can find way to apply your cosmetic dreams into the game! As long as its grounded in the rules. Everybody wants a rocket launcher after all.

-        **Cameo Players:** From time to time, we’ll invite other people to play as NPCs or DM over the interlude from the main story.
