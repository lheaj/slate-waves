---
title: Slate Waves - A Dungeons and Dragons campaign
---
Embark on an electrifying odyssey with Marco, as he hunts for a long-lost comrade amidst the industrial roar of engines and the relentless ticking of clockwork in a smog-choked, nameless world, shattered into fragments. Every island breeds its own brand of cunning, where the shrewd and resourceful rise to power and those who falter are swiftly left to their fates.

Steel yourself to gather unimaginable wealth, unleash a hail of bullets (or fireballs, your choice), topple tyrants, and mingle with society's outcasts. Do it all your way—be it through blazing trails or quiet sabotage. In this tumultuous world, the true treasure lies in the friendships you build.

Sometimes, a friend is all we need. Often, we lose a few along the way, stop seeing them, or feeling their presence. They might look different or speak in ways we no longer recognize. Yet, they often remain, just beneath the surface.
