title: Slate Waves - A Dungeons and Dragons campaign
---
> Sometimes, a friend is all we need. Often, we lose a few along the way, stop seeing them, or feeling their presence. They might look different or speak in ways we no longer recognize. Yet, they often remain, just beneath the surface.



Clues:
- The [[Slate Waves]] - where a century ago, endless seas submerged an old world under **inexplicable and unyielding rains.**

- [[Smallport]] - A town where ***self-made rich merchants have power and safety while the poor make spectacles of their life and death to rise from their desperate state.***

- A weapon merchant ([[Borbor Roctofellor]]) blackmailed into producing weapons for an organization hiding behind metal animal masks, calling themselves "The Menagerie". The weapons: ***bombs that can contain any type of explosive or concoction.***

- [[Perison Island]], a polluted land where The Menagerie supplied its installed ruler [[Violet - The Witch of Perison]], with the test version of ***a mind controlling gas*** in exchange for gold and forced labour to ***produce more weapons**.*

- [[Marco]], a man looking for a lost friend. He mentioned his good hearted companion was investigating on the Menagerie. He then stole a powerful ***wish-granting artefact***, and recently vanished while inexplicably protecting a member of the Menagerie.

- [[Watersafe]] - an underwater city doomed by a mistakenly created ***infectious disease.*** Here, kidnapped younglings were taken by the Menagerie to a powerful Wizard, consuming their lifelines to enrich his own powers. At the Tusseaux ball ***the criminals were looking for Marco's artefact***. A fight erupted, and ***The Wizard nonchalantly attacked the Menagerie and stole the artefact.*** He claimed to be wanting to resolve the mystery of the never ending rains, seeking knowledge, and vanished through a portal leading on a sky island.
