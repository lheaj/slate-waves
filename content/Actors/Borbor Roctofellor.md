>"By me beard, if it’s got a bang that’ll rattle yer bones, odds are I built it—and built it good! An’ don’t be surprised if I’ve already tested the blast meself. Ha!

Borbor Roctofellor, or “Baldie”, is a muscular dwarf with a moon-resembling, helmet-shaped, shiny beyond sight, round, bald head. He’s sporting a gold chained pocket watch and tool’s belt.”

Known for his military prowess, he owns four stands and a large locale near the market square, selling weapons and [[CO7]]-related contraptions. Need a sword? Borbor's got it. A rifle? He's built tons. Throwing stars that explode into hundreds of smaller throwing stars? He'll make it by tomorrow if he doesn’t already have ten.

Borbor detests [[Garabatta]], the green pest who's cornered the riches in [[Smallport]]. He's desperate to dethrone the goblin as the wealthiest figure in town. The two were once business partners but their relationship turned sour when Baldie offered to appease [[Patricia Cotton]]'s dream to travel to [[Watersafe]] and learn its secret. After the submerged town was lost to a mysterious plague, Garabatta blamed Patricia's presumed death to his business partner.

#### Traits
- **Personality**: Borbor isn't a brave fighter brandishing a sword on the front lines but he very much enjoying every little bit of the process that goes into making that very same weapon. He'll talk at length if uninterrupted about explosives or the advantage of using different blends of CO7 to blow up a building.
- **Ideal**: "Me weapon will bring me riches"
- **Bond**: Despite what surfaces as hate, Borbor misses his old partner and friend Garabatta.
- **Flaw**: Greedy
