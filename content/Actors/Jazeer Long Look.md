*"I see you, I see what you are and what you have... and I like it."*

Jazeer is a Tabaxi with the coat of a mountain grey wildcat. She wears an eye-patch where her left sight should have been and partly covering an elongated scar from forehead to chin.

Rescued by [[Gideon Blackbirch]] from a life of petty crimes, she now serves her boss and his business empire as a skilled negotiator and, sometimes, expert extortionist. Jazeer main rival for Gideon's attention has, and still is, [[Vanessa The Vulture]].

*Personality* Jazeer is an intelligent strategists, and considers every word she utter as part of some sort of social game where she always needs to come out on top. Her name derives from her habit of staring intensely at her interlocutor up to the point where they feel uncomfortable. 
*Bond:* She feels the outmost loyalty for Gideon and would risk her life to help him on any task.
*Ideal:* "I am good at what I do, and I live to do it."
*Flaw:* Her extreme competitiveness doesn't let her yield on much in life.
