>*"Heh heh, we'll twist this place, we will, I swears it! Bigport's what they'll name it, oh yes, and Garby shops, plenty o' Garby shops, sprouting up like mushrooms in muck!"*

This middle-aged goblin sports a greying beard adorned by amber eyes. He wears an olive cheque tweed suite and a spotlessly clean red tie.

Garabatta is a sharp merchant with a knack for risky ventures. He’s taken it upon himself to ensure [[Smallport]] becomes "Bigport," a wordplay he loves to repeat. Through his investments, he's propelled Smallport's reputation and wealth.

Garabatta owns six shops in the [[Smallport Market]], an impressive feat achieved in less than two decades, thanks to his business acumen and knack for avoiding trouble. Lately, though, he's been 
seen pacing the market with an unusually nervous look on his not-so-handsome face. 

He has resolved to end his long time hatred of [[Borbor Roctofellor]] in murder, in a legal way off course. 

The once business partner dwarf, is guilty of having offered a job opportunity to [[Patricia Cotton]], Garbatta's childhood one true love. Patricia has left Smallport for [[Watersafe]] to set-up a [[Borbor's Arms and Weapons]] outpost, while he would also pay for the young halfling to study and pursue her own research in the city beneath the Oceans.

Sadly, after the Watersafe's society collapse, Garabatta lost all contacts with Patricia, and blames her presumed death on his previous associate.

#### Traits
**Personality**: Thoughtful and curious. Garbatta is suspicious of others and always on the hunt for the best deal, in every matter, be it business or war.
**Bonds:** His business in Smallport and his undying love for the memory of Patricia.
**Ideal:** "I'll transform Smallport into Bigoport. You'll see Patricia, one day you will see."
**Flaws**: He won't let go of the past.
