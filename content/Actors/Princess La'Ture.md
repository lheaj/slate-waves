>"*I love the silly things, if not for the silly things, life would be all too serious.*"

Princess La'Ture skin is lucid and smooth, a lighter hue of grey when compared to other Shadar-kai. With puffy lips of shaded pink, a small nose and wide smiling eyes, Princess is one to steal many a heart. Her careless nature and lack of focus often leads her right into danger, much to her own blissful ignorance. 

- **Personality**: Princess is vane but not arrogant. She enjoys the ephemeral but is devoid of greed and does not wish to accumulate wealth for herself. On the opposite, Princess thinks of herself as a charitable person, and will strive to do the right thing and share the beauties in her life with those in need. 
- **Ideal**: "The sky is so blue today!"
- **Bond**: Princess loves her sister Vanessa and her boyfriend Gideon but she will follow her heart into helping those in need over her personal bonds.
- **Flaw**: Naive
