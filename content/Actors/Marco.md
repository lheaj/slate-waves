Marco cuts an imposing figure, standing tall in a white shirt with braces holding up a pair of khaki suit trousers. His left arm is a mechanical, oily and ill-maintained, craft out of iron and steel.

Marco carries himself with the demeanor of an intelligent, well-spoken man—calm, humble, and contemplative. He often pauses mid-conversation, lost in thought, his gaze fixed on the distant horizon as if chasing an invisible muse. 

When poised with the option, Marco will chose the most efficient one, even if that same choice would be considered the least morally right one.

Marco’s quest centres around his missing friend, [Elyse](Actors/Elyse.md). He rarely divulges details about her willingly, but when pressed, he shares the following:

- **Her name is Elyse.**
- **She has been a loyal friend for decades, and Marco cares for her as if she were family.**
- **Obsessed with uncovering a secretive organization,** Elyse believes they seek ways to dominate and create a criminal government over the fragmented islands.
- **She is driven to do good,** the most stubborn and intelligent person Marco knows.
- **Her past is fraught with hardship.** Marco rescued her from captors when she was young, forging an unbreakable bond between them.

- **Elyse’s parents abandoned her at birth,** leaving her to grow up in an underfunded orphanage in Smallport. The orphanage, snubbed by wealthy merchants and left to charitable souls, harbored care-givers who exerted power over the children, making their lives miserable.

- **Astio, a generalist teacher at the orphanage,** rented the kids to a sinister mage looking for test subjects, took a scientific interest in Elyse. The unnamed sorcerer tortured her through magical experiments. The tasks weren't sadistic in nature but their impact on Elyse was cruel nonetheless, including mental and physical pain.

- **To cope, Elyse developed bipolar personality disorder.** Elyse is a brilliant, caring, and loving woman, charitable and gentle; her alter ego, Rufi, is a ruthless boy with a fierce desire to change the world by any means. Rufi will stop at nothing to protect Elyse and revels in inflicting pain on those he deems evil.

- **Both Elyse and Rufi view Smallport’s society as deeply flawed.** Elyse seeks change through love and good deeds, while Rufi is bent on punishing the rich, whom he holds responsible for Elyse’s suffering. He is intelligent and cunning, researching secrets across the fragmented islands to achieve his goals.

- **Marco’s connection to Elyse began in childhood.** Visiting the orphanage with his parents, he was horrified by what he saw but couldn’t help the children then. He never forgot Elyse’s face and pained expression. At 15, Marco tricked Astio into believing in a false discovery of a powerful drug that could be re-sold for a profit, rescuing Elyse and fleeing together.

- **For over a decade,** Marco and Elyse have lived as traveling doctors and teachers, helping the poor and sick across the islands.

- **Marco primarily interacts with Elyse,** but he is aware of Rufi’s existence. Rufi distrusts and detests Marco, seeing him as evil and incapable of true love.

As the story unfolds, Marco discovers that the mastermind behind the criminal organizations wreaking havoc across the islands is none other than Rufi. Fearing that the players might abandon the mission to rescue Elyse if they knew the truth, Marco decides to feign ignorance and keep this revelation hidden from them.
