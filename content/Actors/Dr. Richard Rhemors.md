> *"You see it is a curse, a disease. You call them infected, you deem them lost. Yet I am. I reason beyond what is humanly achievable. Then tell me, what am I?"*

Richard Rhemors was among the original architect designing the glass tunnel systems connecting [[Watersafe]] and its constructions. He participated in the ravishing splendour of the submerged city at its peak. Discussing science and arts with the elites and avant-guard in their respective fields of study was what made his life worth.

He was attending a ball at the [[Hotel Tusseaux]] when a horde of [[Brightblood]] infected bursted into the building and consumed all the presents, included him. He has only faint memories of the days that came after. Images of citizens running away, blood on his hands, until one day his conscience awoke. He was staring at a book picturing his past self with a model of [[Watersafe]], smiling proudly. Since then he has been wondering across the tunnels that still are his dearest and only child, writing across their walls in hope to pass his distorted wisdom to others and mark the town with his very existence.

As very few could, he tamed the innate urge to laugh and give in to the aggression and instead harnessed the power of the disease to. Richard, his firmly convinced to having achieved a higher level of existence, beyond human potential. Sadly, in less-apparent yet sinister manner, his mind is just as consumed as the other [[Brightblood]] Infected. 

His limited self-awareness grants him an exceptional control over the body enhancing prowess of [[Brightblood]] and an unexplained ability to direct other infected to follow his command. He does not like to do so, and only resorts to the ability to avoid confrontations or defend himself.

Richard harbours knowledge superior to anyone in the damned underwater city about its structure, secret passages and astonishing sights.

- **Personality**: Richard is a curios intellectual. He is arrogant by nature but strive to tame his urge to preponderate over others and rather seeks debates of opinions. 
- **Ideal**: "Look what I am, is this the way forward?"
- **Bond**:  His child, [[Watersafe]], is all he cares for, and will talk of it for hours on end it let.
- **Flaw**:  Arrogance
