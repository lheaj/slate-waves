> *" I'll spread my wings and see cities and islands, I'll travel far and learn it all, and when I'm done with this world, I'll start over again!*>"*Quote*"

By making Patricia the youngest daughter of a modest [[Smallport]] baker, fate had assigned her the task of coming up the world ladder on her own, using only her determination and curiosity. 

Patricia is of short and small stature, even for an Halfling, but what she lacks in height went straight to her character. There isn't a conversation she won't jump in to explicate her views and challenge her interlocutor into a debate. Her bright blue eyes, the colour a mountain sky, spark with thirst for everything and all they haven't seen yet.

Cotton Flours most affluent visitor and Patricia's childhood friend [[Garabatta]], has always been her ever-present supporter. Since when the two where just smudges of life running around the port city intracate streets catching pigeons and eating scraps, Garabatta has nurtured a deep affection for lively halfling, and was with a broken heart that he saw her leave to pursue her dreams of study and knowledge, directed for [[Watersafe]]. 

Alas, since the tragic twist of event in the submerged city, all contacts have been lost of the Halfing and her presumed death has forever changed Garabatta into a bitter, older, goblin.
