>*Nothing gets me outta bed like the sweet scent of cash... or a kiss from my Baby Doll*

Leader of the Giddys, Gideon is shrewd, lustful and ruthless. Taller than the average draw, he stares investigative at enemies and competitors through his red, iris-less eyes, with unnerving intensity. 

The dark elf is as ambitious as intelligent and some will say he his increasingly losening the boundaries of his humanity in his pursue of power and influence across the [[Slate Waves]]. He has little time for pleasures but when he dips in, he takes a bath. Gideon loves smoking, drinking, and spending his freedom with [[Velina]].

Gideon has encircled himself with an assorted range of skilled criminals and tradesmen, but he trust few better than his advisor [[Jazeer Hakrhah]] and his lethal enforcer [[Vanessa La'Ture]], both equally indispensable to his business growth.
