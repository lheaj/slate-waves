>*"I once tossed an explosive into a C07 depot, almost blew up the entire city! So much fun."*

The remaining heir of a once powerful household, is a young socialite that lives every day as the oceans were about to finally press Watersafe into the sand and end her existence along with the damned city. She enjoys being alive to the fullest, savours every bite and thirsts for new extravagant drinks to add to her memories. Every day is Saturday night to Regina. She wears only party-dresses of eye-popping colours and won't accede to end a night if something "crazy-fun" hasn't happened yet.

Her [[Hotel Tusseaux]], is the only functioning hospitality business within the ocean metropolis. Every year the hotel bathes itself in luxurious decorations, expensed by Regina's herself. The [[Tusseaux Ball]] is seen as a forum where all organizations doing "business" within Watersafe can convene and meet at once. Regina doesn't care if the attendance is heavy on the criminal side, as those people are "fun", and that's what matters. She is broadly regarded as harmless by the real overlord of the underwater metropolis but is, at the same time, protected and cared for by all of them as some sort of mascot. 

During one of her adventures to push life to its limits, she ventured too far out of safe areas within the city and was assaulted by a group of infected. The friends she was carrying along all perished, and she was infected with [[Brightblood]]. [[Dr. Elen Voss]], saved her life through experimental cures but wasn't able to completely revere the effect of the disease on her appearance. Her left eye still heavily carries the mark of the blight, expanding from iris onto a quarter of her face with a visible yellow over-fluorescence lingering on her skin. She has come to accept her new-self albeit still finds it repellent and disgusting and sometimes whispers her belief that no sane man will ever love her again.

- **Personality**: Regina is fun. She likes to have fun, she talks about having fun and will go to great length for the thrill of the night. Her vanity is a distraction, focussing on the superficial to forget about her ruined body and the nightmare she is living every day as a citizen of [[Watersafe]].
- **Ideal**: "I could be dead tomorrow and everything would just end"
- **Bond**: Regina's bonds are ephemeral. She does not feel particular attachment to her hotel or to anything in her life.
- **Flaw**: Self-loathing
