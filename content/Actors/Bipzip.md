>"Z-zZ-zzup-Pp-zz-ZZ-zp-P-ppZ-z-Bpzp"

Ever-diligent in his quest to keep the ship's deck immaculate, the silver-plated automaton is the epitome of a perfect sailor. Bipzip's mastery of navigation is unparalleled, and his passion manifests as an incessant stream of commentary, mercifully reduced to cryptic electrical murmurs, for he lacks a mouth. 

These sounds are little more than background static to most of the crew, but not to [[Marco]]. Having been subjected to countless hours of the warforged's ceaseless "chatter," Marco has unwillingly become fluent in Bipzip's peculiar language of hums and beeps.

When Marco bought the ship from a used-boat retailer, the mechanical man refused to part with the vehicle, and was somehow "sold" along with it to Marco.

#### Traits
- **Personality**: Bipzip bips a lot but doesn't talk much. He is very particular about keeping the ship in top shape and will anger if the deck is stained by careless passengers.
- **Ideal**: Bip
- **Bond**: [[Marco]] is perhaps the only person understanding him and that feeling of being comprehended is hard to find anywhere else.
- **Flaw**: Bipzip cares for his ship down to an unhealthy level of maniacal attention to detail.
