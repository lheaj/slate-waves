**Borbor Roctofellor “Baldie”:** _“Borbor, is a muscular dwarf with a moon-resembling, helmet-shaped, shiny beyond sight, round, bald head. He’s sporting a gold chained pocket watch and tool’s belt.”_

Known for his military prowess, he owns four stands and a large locale near the market square, selling weapons and [[CO7]]-related contraptions. Need a sword? Borbor's got it. A rifle? He's built tons. Throwing stars that explode into hundreds of smaller throwing stars? He'll make it by tomorrow if he doesn’t already have ten.

Borbor detests [[Garabatta]], the green pest who's cornered the riches in [[Smallport]]. He's desperate to dethrone the goblin as the wealthiest figure in town.
