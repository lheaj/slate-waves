A collection of the key ongoing storylines.

## Marco And His Missing Friend

In the bustling port town of [[Smallport]], a notice pinned to a board by a tall marksman named [[Marco]] appealed to volunteers for assistance. The reward offered was passage aboard a swift ship navigating through the treacherous [[Slate Waves]]. The task at hand was to investigate the disappearance of a missing person. Marco referred to the missing woman as "[[Elyse]]," describing her as an attractive woman in her twenties, with short, raven-black hair. Marco remained tight-lipped about the circumstances surrounding her disappearance.

## The Nept And Its Sickness

The once-pristine white corals of [[Nept]], sanctuary to the wandering Genasi nomads of this altered world, have succumbed to sprouting mysterious illnesses creeping in from beyond its borders. Among the Genasi dwelling within the alabaster reefs, symptoms of these affliction have surfaced, ranging from severe respiratory ailments to bizarre transformations, where their skin takes on the appearance of corroded metal. Worse still, some have exhibited erratic, violent behaviours paired with an unsettling fluorescence coursing through their blood. As a last hope, the tribe resumed it tradition to send its youth beyond the safety of their people, to explore the [[Slate Waves]] in search of answers and remedies for this insidious plague.

## The Pretty Princess Gone Missing

Few commanded the same respect as [[Gideon Blackbirch]] within the back-alleys of [[Smallport]]. As time passed his influence webbed through the [[Slate Waves]] among smuggler ships and weapon traders. Yet, with the success of his sprawling criminal empire, came new and powerful enemies. When a person dear to him, one he calls "Pretty Princess", was seemingly victim of kidnapping, Gideon mobilized his resources to find her, enlisting his most prestigious enforcer [[Vanessa La'Ture]].

## The Wizard

Whispers of a powerful mage linger on the lips of criminals and rulers. The extent of his power lost in a fog of inexplicable actions. His previous protégé' [[Violet - The Witch of Perison]], revealed his obsession with eternal-life and hinted to his involvement in the disappearance of young adults across the [[Slate Waves]].
